# Fidelity Rules

## Content Preservation

Preserve book content exactly as printed or as OCR can reliably transcribe it. Do not:

- rewrite or improve sentences
- translate text
- normalize old spelling
- add missing words based on context
- remove authorial repetition
- merge or split sentences based on preference
- silently correct names, numbers, dates, equations, citations, or punctuation

When OCR is uncertain, keep the best recognized text and add a note such as `uncertain word near line 12`; do not guess.

## Artifact Removal

Ignore marks introduced by scanning or distribution when they are clearly not book content:

- translucent watermarks or site names overlaid on every page
- color calibration blocks and solid color bands
- scanner bed edges, shadows, skew borders, speckles, and bleed-through
- crop marks and file-management stamps
- repeated non-content labels added outside the printed page area

Do not remove printed publisher marks, copyright text, bookplates, page numbers, headings, footers, marginalia, captions, diagrams, or notes unless the user explicitly says to omit them.

## Formatting Freedom

EPUB formatting may differ from the scanned page layout when needed for readability. Allowed changes include:

- reflowing body paragraphs
- using semantic headings instead of matching exact font size
- converting printed lists or tables into HTML lists or tables
- placing footnotes at the end of a chapter or page

Formatting changes must not alter text content or reading order.
